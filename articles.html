From Resentment To Pride

October 2, 2023

As a third culture kid, Lena grew up in so many places, she has experienced a tumultuous journey when it comes to reconciling with her Asian cultural origin and her upbringing in 9 different places. There was a time when she grappled with feelings of resentment and embarrassment toward her heritage, but through self-reflection, she discovered that it was merely a phase of growth. It is not uncommon for third-culture kids like her to struggle with finding their place in the complex web of cultural identities. While she was fortunate enough to have been exposed to both her Asian heritage and other cultures, she found herself longing to fit in with her American peers. This desire to assimilate led to moments of embarrassment and sometimes even a sense of shame towards her Asian background.

Everything I Never Told You

November 27, 2023

In the novel "Everything I Never Told You" by Celeste Ng, the Chinese American family faces numerous struggles that stem from their complex identity and the pressures they face in American society during the 1970s. The narrative sheds light on themes such as racism, cultural assimilation, gender roles, and the burden of expectations. One of the primary struggles of the Chinese-American family in the novel revolves around racism and cultural discrimination. The characters face constant prejudice and scrutiny due to their ethnic background. This discrimination is evident in the experience of Marilyn Lee, the mother of the family, who faces discrimination while pursuing her dream of becoming a doctor. She is often undermined and overlooked by her white classmates and professors, leading to feelings of isolation and self-doubt.
